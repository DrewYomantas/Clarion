# Clarion — Company North Star

> Agents must read this file before proposing any new initiative, feature,
> integration, or architectural change. If a proposal falls outside the
> boundaries defined here, escalate to the CEO rather than proceeding.

---

## MISSION

Clarion converts fragmented client feedback into governance signals and
partner-level decision intelligence for law firms.

---

## TARGET MARKET

Solo and small law firms.

---

## CORE VALUE PROPOSITION

Clarion aggregates client feedback sources (reviews, surveys, internal
notes) and converts them into structured governance insights for law firm
partners.

---

## CORE PRODUCT

Client feedback governance intelligence for law firms.

---

## NON-GOALS

Clarion will not expand into:

- Generic CRM systems
- Legal practice management software
- Marketing automation platforms
- Consulting services

Any proposal that moves the product toward these areas is out of scope.
Agents must escalate to the CEO rather than implementing or recommending
such work.

---

## ENFORCEMENT RULE FOR AGENTS

Before proposing or executing any new initiative, feature, integration, or
architectural change, agents must ask:

1. Does this serve solo or small law firms?
2. Does this directly support the core product (client feedback →
   governance intelligence)?
3. Does this fall outside the Non-Goals list above?

If the answer to (3) is yes, or if the answers to (1) or (2) are no,
**stop and escalate to the CEO**. Do not proceed.
