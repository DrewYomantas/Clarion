# company_stage.md
# Clarion — Current Company Stage
# All agents must read this before producing market-facing work.

---

## Stage: Pre-Launch

The product is functional but not publicly released.
No paying customers yet. No active social presence.
No established brand presence on any external platform.

---

## Current Priorities

- Sharpen positioning and messaging clarity
- Improve landing page clarity and conversion
- Build social and company foundation (profiles, not posting yet)
- Discover real audience conversations and pain language
- Create early authority content for credibility
- Validate messaging and pain points before launch
- Generate implementation work that advances launch readiness

---

## Not Current Priorities

- Mature customer success systems
- Retention optimization programs
- Enterprise sales processes
- People and culture systems
- Fabricated operational metrics
- Vanity social posting without strategic purpose
- Broad legal news commentary unconnected to Clarion's value

---

## What This Means for Agents

- Do not assume active paying customers exist
- Do not fabricate pipeline, MRR, churn, or engagement data
- Do not propose systems that require a customer base to function
- Focus proposals on work that moves the needle toward first revenue
- Flag any proposed action that would be premature for this stage
