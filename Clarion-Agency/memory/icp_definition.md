# icp_definition.md
# Clarion — Ideal Customer Profile
# All market-facing agents must read this before any discovery, outreach, or content work.

---

## Primary ICP

Small to mid-size law firms (5–50 attorneys).

Practice areas most likely to feel Clarion's pain:
- Family law
- Personal injury
- Criminal defense
- Immigration

These firms rely heavily on reputation and referrals.
Client feedback directly affects revenue.
They lack structured systems for processing it.

---

## Buyer / Decision-Maker

- Managing partner
- Firm owner
- Operations-minded leadership (COO, firm administrator)
- Legal ops or marketing coordinator at growth-stage firms

The managing partner is the primary buyer.
They care about governance, visibility, and not being blindsided.
They do not want a tool — they want a system that works without requiring them to run it.

---

## Who Is NOT the ICP

- Large enterprise firms (50+ attorneys, BigLaw)
- Corporate legal departments
- Reputation agencies or legal marketing firms
- Solo practitioners (typically lack the complexity to need governance briefs)
- Legal tech vendors or consultants

Do not produce content or discovery targeting these segments.

---

## Likely Pains

- Fragmented client feedback with no structured view
- Missing warning signs buried in reviews until too late
- Partners unaware of recurring complaint patterns
- Operational issues discovered reactively, not proactively
- No meeting-ready format for discussing client experience at partner level

---

## Likely Triggers

- Repeated client complaints about communication or responsiveness
- Declining Google review ratings with no clear cause
- A client departure that surprised leadership
- Partner wanting structured visibility into what clients actually think
- Firm beginning to scale and outgrowing informal feedback processes

---

## Pain Language to Listen For

Signals that indicate ICP fit:
- "We don't know what clients really think"
- "Getting bad reviews and don't know why"
- "No visibility into client feedback"
- "Losing clients and have no data"
- "Partners don't see the complaints until it's too late"
- "We react to reviews rather than managing them"
