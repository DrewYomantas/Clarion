# founder_escalation.md
# Clarion — Founder Escalation Rules
# Version: 1.0 | 2026-03-12

---

## Purpose

Defines conditions that require immediate CEO attention regardless of cadence.
Any agent that detects an escalation trigger must surface it prominently in their
report under FOUNDER ESCALATIONS — not buried in findings, not in PROPOSED ACTIONS.

Chief of Staff must surface all active escalations in EXCEPTIONS REQUIRING CEO ATTENTION
in the next CEO brief, even if the brief is being generated the same cycle.

---

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate IMMEDIATELY when any of the following occur:

SALES / PIPELINE
- A prospect firm requests a call or meeting
- A prospect firm requests pricing information
- A prospect firm requests a pilot analysis
- A completed pilot brief is ready for review
- A prospect moves from pilot_complete → ready to discuss subscription

PARTNERSHIPS
- Any firm, platform, or individual proposes a partnership, referral arrangement,
  or distribution deal
- Any bar association, legal tech association, or industry body expresses interest

PRESS / MEDIA
- A journalist, blogger, or publication asks about Clarion
- A podcast or event invitation arrives
- Any unsolicited media mention is discovered

ENTERPRISE / HIGH-VALUE
- A law firm with 50+ attorneys expresses interest
- A multi-location firm or legal group expresses interest
- An investor or VC asks about Clarion

LEGAL / SECURITY
- Any legal question about Clarion's data handling, liability, or terms
- Any security vulnerability or concern is reported
- Any compliance question from a prospect or customer

---

## Escalation Format

In sales reports, use this block:

```
FOUNDER ESCALATIONS
  Firm / Source: [Name or identifier]
  Trigger:       [call request | pricing | pilot request | pilot complete |
                  partnership | press | enterprise | legal | investor]
  Detail:        [One sentence — what happened]
  Urgency:       [High | Critical]
  Recommended:   [What the CEO should do next — one sentence]
```

In CEO brief, Chief of Staff surfaces all open escalations under
EXCEPTIONS REQUIRING CEO ATTENTION, ranked by urgency.

---

## Response SLA

Critical escalations (call request, pricing, pilot complete, legal, security):
  CEO should respond within 24 hours.

High escalations (partnership, press, enterprise interest):
  CEO should respond within 48 hours.

---

## What NOT to Escalate

Do not use founder escalation for:
- Routine outreach drafts awaiting Level 2 approval
- Lead research completions
- Pipeline updates with no external interest signal
- Content drafts
- Normal office health issues

These belong in PROPOSED ACTIONS or PIPELINE UPDATES, not FOUNDER ESCALATIONS.
