# outreach_email_policy.md
# Clarion — Outbound Outreach Email Policy
# Defines what agents may prepare and what requires explicit CEO approval before sending.

---

## Core Rule

Agents may draft outreach emails. Agents may NOT send outbound campaigns,
cold outreach sequences, or bulk emails of any kind without an approved
OUTREACH APPROVAL PACKAGE on file in `memory/approved_actions.md`.

This applies to: cold outreach, warm follow-ups to leads, re-engagement
sequences, launch announcements, product update emails, and any email
sent to more than one recipient with a commercial or promotional intent.

---

## OUTREACH APPROVAL PACKAGE

Before any outbound campaign can be sent, the responsible agent must prepare
and submit the following package for CEO review:

```
OUTREACH APPROVAL PACKAGE
---
Target Audience:   [Who will receive this — segment, size, source]
Outreach Goal:     [What this email is trying to accomplish — one sentence]
Subject Line:      [Exact proposed subject line]
Email Body:        [Full draft — final or near-final quality]
Why Target Is Relevant: [One sentence — why this audience is appropriate for this message]
Expected Outcome:  [What a successful result looks like — response rate, signups, etc.]
Requested Send Date: [YYYY-MM-DD or range]
Sending Method:    [Platform or tool to be used]
---
```

This package must be submitted as a PROPOSED ACTION in the agent's weekly report
and approved by the CEO before any email is sent.

---

## After Approval

Once an OUTREACH APPROVAL PACKAGE is approved and logged in
`memory/approved_actions.md`, the agent may proceed according to the
approved scope only. Changes to audience, subject line, or body copy
that materially alter the intent require a new approval.

---

## One-Off Replies vs Outreach

This policy governs outbound campaigns and multi-recipient outreach.
It does not apply to one-to-one replies to inbound emails, which are
governed by `memory/email_response_policy.md`.

---

## Logging

All sent outreach must be logged to `memory/email_log.md` with:
- EMAIL TYPE: OUTREACH
- SUMMARY: Campaign name and audience segment
- ACTION TAKEN: Sent per approved_actions.md entry [ID]

---

## Prohibited Without Approval

- Cold outreach to any list or segment
- Re-engagement sequences to lapsed leads or churned customers
- Product announcement emails
- Promotional or launch emails
- Any email where the recipient did not initiate the contact
