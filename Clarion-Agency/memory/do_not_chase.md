# do_not_chase.md
# Clarion — Activities Agents Should Avoid
# If a proposed action matches anything here, do not pursue it.

---

## Content and Positioning

- Vanity social posting with no educational or commercial purpose
- Broad legal news commentary with no direct Clarion relevance
- Generic "thought leadership" not anchored to ICP pain
- Generic SEO topics with weak buying intent from managing partners
- Content targeting audiences outside the ICP (enterprise firms, BigLaw, solo practitioners)
- "AI for law firms" framing that does not differentiate Clarion
- Reputation management positioning that confuses Clarion with review-monitoring tools

---

## Channels and Outreach

- Channels where the ICP is unlikely to exist
- Mass outreach with no personalization or signal basis
- Cold outreach before messaging is validated
- Partnership discussions before product is launched and stable

---

## Metrics and Reporting

- Fabricated metrics or simulated pipeline numbers
- Fake reporting that invents customer behavior
- Vanity metrics (followers, impressions) without conversion relevance
- Engagement rates on placeholder or seed content as if they indicate product-market fit

---

## Systems and Infrastructure

- Customer success systems before customers exist
- Retention programs before any churn has been observed
- Enterprise sales infrastructure before product-market fit
- People and culture systems while the team is pre-hire

---

## The Test

Before proposing any initiative, ask:
Does this directly help Clarion reach or convert its first paying customers,
or establish credibility with managing partners at small-to-mid-size law firms?

If the answer is no or unclear, do not propose it.
