# division_lead_approvals.md
# Clarion Agent Office — Division Lead Approval Register
# Version: 1.0 | 2026-03-12

---

## Purpose

Tracks actions approved by division leads that do NOT require CEO approval.
These are Level 2 actions per memory/delegated_authority_matrix.md.

Division leads are humans, not agents. An agent may not approve its own actions.
CEO does not need to review entries here unless they surface in the CEO brief.

The runner reads this file for Level 2 execution alongside approved_actions.md for Level 3.

---

## Format

Each block uses this structure:

  ## DLA-NNN
  ACTION: [what to do — one sentence]
  DIVISION: [Comms & Content | Sales | Market Intelligence | Product Insight]
  APPROVED_BY: [name or role of division lead]
  DATE: [YYYY-MM-DD]
  STATUS: [staged | approved | in_progress | completed | blocked]
  NOTES: [optional context]
