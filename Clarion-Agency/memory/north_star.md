# Clarion — North Star Metric
# Version: 1.0 | 2026-03-12

---

## Current North Star

**Meaningful conversations with managing partners about client feedback problems.**

---

## Definition

A "meaningful conversation" is:
- A real exchange (not a cold message with no reply) with a managing partner,
  firm owner, or operations-minded law firm leader
- About the specific problem Clarion solves: fragmented client feedback, lack
  of partner-level visibility, signals hidden in public reviews
- Where the prospect expresses a genuine interest in understanding more, seeing
  a pilot, or discussing how the problem applies to their firm

A conversation is NOT meaningful if:
- It is a generic "thanks for reaching out" reply
- It is a clear rejection with no engagement
- It is with someone outside the ICP (non-law firm, wrong practice area, wrong role)

---

## Why This Metric

At pre-launch stage, no ARR exists. No customer count. No activation data.
The only leading indicator of future revenue is: are we talking to the right people?

Meaningful conversations precede pilots. Pilots precede calls. Calls precede customers.
Every piece of outbound work — research, drafts, pilot analyses, content — should be
evaluated by whether it increases the likelihood of more meaningful conversations.

---

## What Moves This Metric

Direct movers:
- Qualifying and contacting ICP-fit firms
- Completing pilot analyses and sharing findings
- Publishing content that attracts managing partners
- Warm outreach through LinkedIn or communities
- Founder-led walkthroughs and calls

Indirect movers:
- LinkedIn presence and educational content (builds awareness)
- Community participation in legal operations discussions
- Competitive positioning that differentiates from reputation management tools

Does NOT move this metric:
- Follower counts
- Post impressions
- Agent activity with no external output
- Outreach to non-ICP contacts
- Content that speaks to the wrong audience

---

## Agent Responsibility

Every agent report should implicitly or explicitly connect its work to this metric.
Chief of Staff should flag work that has no clear path to meaningful conversations
as stage-inappropriate or misaligned with commercial priority.
